1. How many types of conditions are available in conditional formatting on Excel?

ans - There are 5 types of conditional formatting visualizations available - Background Color Shading , Foreground Color Shading, Data Bars, Icons and Values. 

2. How to insert border in Excel with Format Cells dialog?

ans - step1 - Select the cell(s) where you want to add the border.
step2 - Click the Border list arrow from the Home tab.
step3 - Select a border type.

3. How to Format Numbers as Currency in Excel?

ans - Within the Format Cell window there is the option to change the symbol you use for currency.Click on the drop-down menu arrows to the right of Symbol. A list of hundreds of different currency symbols will appear. Select the symbol you prefer.

4. What are the steps to format numbers in Excel with the Percent style?

ans - step1 - Use the keyboard shortcut Control + Shift + Enter
step 2 - Select "Percentage" from the dropdown on the home tab of the ribbon
step3 - Click the % button in the Number section on the home tab of the ribbon
step4 - Control + 1 > Number > Percentage
